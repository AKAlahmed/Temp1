FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers

Bidsfy is a B2B platform that allows suppliers to create professional digital profile pages. Think of it as a digital business card on steroids - you can showcase your company, products, collect reviews, and share everything via a single link.

After signing up, you'll go through a simple setup wizard to add your business information, logo, products, and contact details. Once complete, you'll get a unique link (like bidsfy.com/profile/your-business) that you can share with anyone.

Your profile is only visible to the public after you subscribe. Before subscribing, you can still create and edit your profile, but only you can see it. Once subscribed, anyone with your profile link can view it.

We use Tap Payment which supports all major credit cards, debit cards, and local payment methods in the MENA region including KNET, mada, Benefit, and more.

When your subscription expires, your profile becomes invisible to the public immediately. Your data is safely stored, and you can renew anytime to make your profile visible again.

Absolutely! You can edit your profile, add or remove products, and update your information anytime from your dashboard. Changes are reflected immediately.

Other registered users on our platform can leave reviews and ratings on your profile. This helps build trust and credibility with potential clients. Each user can leave one review per profile.
